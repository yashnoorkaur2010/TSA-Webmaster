Oura Ring

The Oura Ring was founded by a health technology company in Finland in 2013. Ever since then, the Oura Ring has been improved and advanced into its current compact of features and look.

What is the Oura Ring?

The Oura Ring is a smart ring that provides users with biometric data. With such a sleek and minimalistic design, not only can this device be worn everywhere no matter the occasion but also very convenient. The Oura Ring also includes many features such as sleep data, heart rate monitoring, activity tracking, temperature sensing, and even blood oxygen levels. On top of this, the ring’s features are also a lot more advanced such as the sleep data feature. The Oura Ring also has a very long battery life making things a lot easier for users. The ring’s app is also very user-friendly and includes features such as the Oura Circles which helps the community connect.

The Oura Ring also has many size and color options, making it customizable for every customer.

Additional Information

The cost of the Oura Ring varies based on color and model types but the range is between $299-$499 and a $6 monthly payment for the app.

The features in the Oura Ring indclude sleep/rest monitering, women's health biometrics, stress, activity/movement, and metabolic health.

You can buy the Oura Ring in many different shades: Midnight, Cloud, Petal, Tide, Silver, Gold, Stealth, Rose Gold, Brushed Silver, Black.
